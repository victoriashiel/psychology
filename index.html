About

About Victoria

Victoria Shiel is an English-speaking psychologist based in Dubai with over five years of clinical experience across the UK, Gibraltar, and Spain. I hold a BSc in Psychology from University College London (UCL) and have worked with adults and adolescents in private clinical settings throughout my career.

My therapeutic approach integrates Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Compassion Focused Therapy (CFT) within a trauma-informed, client-led framework. I believe therapy should feel both safe and productive. A space where you can better understand yourself, build practical tools, and create meaningful, sustainable change.

Therapeutic Approach

My work is grounded in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Compassion Focused Therapy (CFT), which allows me to tailor each session to you as an individual.
